Get Ready for a Very Cherry Happy Hour

Sugar is so last century. Taste the new generation of carbonation with this 5-calorie recipe for our Cherry Rye Bubble Over.
CHERRY RYE BUBBLE OVER
Ingredients:

  • 4 oz Bai Bubbles Bolivia Black Cherry
  • 3 maraschino cherries
  • 1 blood orange slice
  • 2 oz rye whiskey
  • ice
Directions: Muddle maraschino cherries and slice of blood orange. Add 2 oz Rye whiskey and stir. Strain into glass over ice. Top with Bubbles Bolivia Black Cherry. Garnish with maraschino cherry.
